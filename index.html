Places must visit

I had visited Bangalore in 2019 in August.

Bangalore is sometimes referred to as the “Silicon Valley of India” (or “IT capital of India”) because of its role as the nation's leading information technology (IT) exporter. Indian technological organisations ISRO, Infosys, Wipro and HAL are headquartered in the city.



I had visited Seattle in Feburary,2023

Seattle is known for being the coffee capital, a home base of big technology companies, and the origin of grunge music. It is home to iconic landmarks like the Space Needle and Pike Place Market. Seattle is also famous for its lush greenery as the emerald city and the delicious food, beer, and wine on offer.



I had visited San Diego in 2022 in April.

San Diego is renowned for its idyllic climate, 70 miles of pristine beaches and a dazzling array of world-class family attractions. Popular attractions include the world-famous San Diego Zoo and San Diego Zoo Safari Park, SeaWorld San Diego and LEGOLAND California.

Review on the movies

Avengers Endgame
Exellent film to experience MCU ride

Caught between time traveling, purple aliens, and a healthy dollop of nostalgia, Avengers: Endgame has proved itself to be the best MCU film ever made, as the heroes really are in the endgame now. To truly back this claim, it will require watching the entire three-hour film and analyzing it from start to finish

The Lion King
The visuals were stunning and breath taking

The story is emotional and moving. Based on Shakespeare's classic play Hamlet, The Lion King follows a time-tested narrative and tells it in a completely new and entertaining way. We relate to the characters in a very strong and personal way. They're instantly recognizable and relatable to a lot of people
